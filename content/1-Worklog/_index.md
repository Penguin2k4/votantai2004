**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:
**Week 1:** [Environment integration, learning regulations, connecting with the AWS community, and getting familiar with tools](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [Team formation, practicing basic AWS administration, and getting familiar with the Console interface & CLI](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [Practicing Amazon VPC network configuration, building a basic model for Phishing URL detection.](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [Training and testing AI/ML algorithms for Phishing URLs and completing AWS Module 3 practice labs.](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [Optimizing the Phishing URL model, and completing AWS Module 3 & 4 practice labs.](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Deploying the AI model to the public and learning Modules 4 & 5.](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Fine-tuning the AI model and completing AWS Module 5.](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [Programming to finalize project features and testing scenarios.](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [Researching and translating an AWS blog.](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [Learning AWS Cloud9 and synthesizing group articles.](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [Fixing errors, finalizing records, and writing the report.](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [Internship summary and portal submission.](1.12-week12/)
