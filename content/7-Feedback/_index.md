### Overall Assessment

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment at the company is highly friendly, open, and professional. The members of the First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ) project were always ready to enthusiastically support me when I faced difficulties, even outside of office hours. The comfortable and well-equipped workspace helped me optimize my concentration.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
My mentor provided exceptionally dedicated guidance, explaining issues thoroughly and always encouraging a proactive mindset to ask questions. The team admin enthusiastically assisted with administrative procedures and documentation, creating the best conditions for interns to focus on our professional tasks.

**3. Alignment between Work and Major**  
The assigned tasks closely aligned with the specialized knowledge I acquired at university, while also expanding into many new practical aspects. As a result, I have not only solidified my theoretical foundation but also sharpened my applied thinking and practical skills.

**4. Opportunities for Learning & Skill Development**  
Throughout my work process, I have cultivated many crucial skills such as using professional project management tools and gaining knowledge about AWS services. These practical experiences have given me a clearer perspective and better orientation for my upcoming career path.

**5. Culture & Team Spirit**  
The company possesses a highly positive work culture: promoting respect and transparency, and maintaining a serious work ethic while preserving a sociable atmosphere. Team spirit and mutual support are very evident among team members. This gives me a true sense of belonging to the collective and making real contributions, even as an intern.

**6. Policies / Benefits for Interns**  
The company has highly satisfactory intern support policies, including time flexibility when necessary. Being given the opportunity to participate in events is a massive privilege, helping me broaden my horizons and continuously update my knowledge.

### Other Questions

- **What are you most satisfied with during your internship?**  
  Getting exposed to office regulations and working styles, which are things I had not encountered during my academic studies, along with the impressive facilities.
- **What do you think the company needs to improve for future interns?**  
  I hope to be able to work at the office more frequently. Sometimes, when I registered to work at the office, it took a bit long to get approved.
- **If introducing to friends, would you recommend they intern here? Why?**  
  I would definitely recommend it. This is an ideal, dynamic, and highly experiential environment for students to gain hands-on experience and comprehensively develop both hard and soft skills.

### Proposals & Desires

- **Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?**  
  I hope that the organizers can approve more students so they can experience this great environment.
- **Do you want to continue this program in the future?**  
  I sincerely wish to continue accompanying the program and hope to have the opportunity to become a full-time employee of the company after completing my internship.
- **Other feedback:**  
  I would like to express my sincere gratitude to the management board, the FCAJ team, and especially my mentor for always being patient, supportive, and creating the best developmental conditions for me during this past period.
